# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** May 1, 2026

---

Welcome to Ace Widgets.

Ace Widgets is a tennis widget app for iPhone that displays ATP and WTA tour information — rankings, calendars, Grand Slam schedules, and season data — directly on your Home Screen and Lock Screen.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

The app includes an optional profile section where you may enter a display name and an email address. Both fields are entirely optional — you are not required to fill them in to use the app or access any features. This information is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted anywhere.

We do not collect analytics, usage data, crash reports, device identifiers, location data, or any other information. There is no backend server, no database, and no third-party SDKs in the app.

## 2. Subscriptions and Purchases

In-app subscriptions are handled entirely by Apple via StoreKit. We do not process, store, or have access to your payment information or Apple ID. All billing is managed directly by Apple. Please refer to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) for details on how Apple handles purchase data.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

The name and email you optionally enter in your profile are used solely for display purposes within the app. We do not use this information for marketing, analytics, or any other purpose.

## 4. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your information with anyone. There are no third-party analytics tools, advertising networks, or data brokers involved in the operation of this app. The only external service the app interacts with is Apple's StoreKit for subscription management.

## 5. Your Rights

You have full control over any data stored by the app. You can view, edit, or clear your profile information at any time within the app. Deleting the app from your device permanently removes all locally stored data. Since there is no backend, there is no server-side data to request deletion of.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Ace Widgets is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 7. Third-Party Data and Trademarks

The tennis data displayed in this app (rankings, schedules, and related tour information) is sourced from publicly available ATP and WTA data. Ace Widgets is an independent app and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to the ATP Tour, WTA Tour, or any Grand Slam tournament. All tour names, logos, and trademarks are the property of their respective owners.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this document. Your continued use of the app after any changes means you accept the updated policy.
