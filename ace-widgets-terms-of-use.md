## 1. About the App

Ace Widgets is a tennis widget app for iPhone that provides Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets covering ATP and WTA tour content, including rankings, calendars, Grand Slam information, and season data. The App does not provide live scores or real-time data. All tennis information is sourced from publicly available ATP and WTA data and updated periodically. The App does not send push notifications.
